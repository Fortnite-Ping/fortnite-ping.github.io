Fortnite Ping Checker is a tiny Fortnite ping app that lets you find the ping within your machine and Fortnite servers. Fortnite is a free to play online video game developed by the American video game company EpicGames, based in North Carolina, released in July of 2017. It is categorized as an online shooter, survival and sandbox game.

There are different modes of play.

Fortnite: Save the World is a game mode where up to four players can team up and fight off against hordes of zombies and build fortifications for survival. As of 2019, this is the only game mode that features the buy to play model.

Fortnite Creative is a build your own world customizable mode where players are encouraged to create different maps, constructions and battle arenas.
